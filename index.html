About Shawn

Shawn Peters is a generative artist and creative coder based in Newfoundland and Labrador. His work explores the intersection of technology, mathematics, and aesthetics through algorithmic processes, primarily using p5.js.

Artist Statement

Shawn’s creative practice is rooted in structured randomness and emergent patterns, blending deterministic structures with chaotic elements. He creates dynamic visuals that range from still images to interactive installations. His work emphasizes computational aesthetics, audience interaction, and the distinction between generative art and AI-generated works.
